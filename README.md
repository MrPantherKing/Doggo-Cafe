# Doggo Café
<p>Doggo Café is a warm, welcoming, and responsive café website designed to celebrate great coffee, comforting food, and the joy of good company — both human and canine. Showcase your café online with style and personality to attract more guests who appreciate a cozy, pet-friendly space. Whether you use Doggo Café’s template as-is or customize it to fit your brand, you’ll get a polished, professional result that reflects your café’s charm.</p>
<p>Featuring a sleek navigation bar, vibrant photo gallery, detailed menu, customer testimonials, and integrated Google Maps location — Doggo Café helps your visitors easily find, explore, and connect with you. The layout is lightweight and fully responsive, ensuring a seamless browsing experience on all modern devices and browsers. Simple, inviting, and thoughtfully designed, Doggo Café blends creativity with usability — helping your café grow its community, one cup at a time.</p>
